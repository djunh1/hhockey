General Questions

What is Hopewell Hockey

  • Hopewell Hockey was born from a simple idea: hockey sticks are too expensive. After some research, we discovered that much of the cost associated with the top end sticks comes from middlemen and marketing. So, we decided to do something about it. At Hopewell Hockey, we’ve engineered a superior hockey stick. By selling sell it directly to our customers, we can help make hockey affordable again. We started Hopewell Hockey, so that everyone can enjoy the sport that we love.

Why should I buy a Hopewell Hockey stick?

  • Sticks wear out, break, or simply aren’t what you were looking for. Maybe the flex is too stiff, or you don’t like the release of the puck with the new type of curve. Perhaps you just want to try something new without spending your vacation money to do it. Whatever the case might be, shop with confidence knowing you’ll get something light, responsive, and well priced.

Do you only sell hockey sticks?

  • The short answer is yes, but just for now. Our longer-term goal is to be the one-stop shop for hockey equipment. We’re also developing next-generation, first-to-market products for the sport. Stay tuned.

Our Sticks

How do our sticks compare to those of major brands?

  • Our sticks are as light and responsive as the top models from major brands. These companies pour millions of dollars into 'research' and marketing in order to sell features like 'multiple kick points'. By selling directly to customers and avoiding gimmicks, we're able to deliver a superior product for half the price.

Can I customize a stick?

  • We offer a wide variety of stick flexes and curves. However, we currently do not fully customize sticks. If the stick you want is out of stock, or there is a configuration you are looking for, we would love to hear from you. Visit our contact Page

Purchasing our Products

I live outside of the United States, can I make a purchase?

  • We do not support purchases outside of the United States. In the future, we will allow purchases from other countries.

What is included in my purchase statement?

  • When a card is charged, a statement descriptor will appear on your statement. This is simply a description of what you just purchased. Our descriptor is HOCKEY EQUIPMENT; you will see this on your monthly credit card statement. Please note that our card issuing bank can automatically include other account information to show your statement. None of this information is stored on our servers and is handled entirely by Stripe.

Can I buy your sticks at a pro shop?

  • Not currently, but we plan to work with local pro shops in the future. If you are a pro shop owner who is interested in selling our sticks, please contact us at info@hopewellhockey.com.

What payment methods can I use?

  • We accept all major credit cards - Mastercard, Visa, and American Express. We do not accept cash, CODs or checks online.

Is my Credit Card information Secure?

  • We have partnered with Stripe in order to provide you with a reliable, secure way to shop on our site. With Stripe, communications with their services are secure, your information never touches our backend systems, and we do not store any sensitive information about you. For more information about Stripe, please visit their site.

How do I get Shipping Notifications?

  • When you make a purchase, you will immediately receive an order number via email. If you don’t see this email, check your spam folder and modify your whitelist to include hopewellhockey.com. If for some reason you do not receive a confirmation email, please contact us at info@hopewellhockey.com and an associate will get back to you within 48 hours.

    Currently we do not provide shipping tracking information for our first batch of sticks. If you would like one, we can provide it to you if you send us an email at info@hopewellhockey.com.

Problems

My stick broke/arrived damaged. Do I get a refund?

  • Our warranty is 30 days from the day you receive your stick in the mail. If your stick arrives damaged or breaks within the warranty time period, we will refund your purchase cost. Please check out our Warranty Claims for more information.

I entered the wrong shipping address. How do I change it?

  • No problem. Contact us at info@hopewellhockey.com. Include your order number in the subject line and an associate will get back to you within 48 hours.

My Account

I got locked out of my account, how do I recover it?

  • You are able to recover the account by resetting your password. Navigate to the login page, and click ‘forgot password’. An email will be sent to the address you registered with the account.

    If you are logging into an account using facebook or twitter, you will not be able to reset your password through hopewellhockey.com. Please consult with your facebook or twitter account for help with your password.

Will Hopewell Hockey post to my facebook or Twitter?

  • Currently, we will not be posting any content to social media from this site.

My account has been compromised, what do I do?

  • If you suspect your account has been compromised, please email us at info@hopewellhockey.com. We can suspend, lock or delete your account. We will not provide sensitive information including an account's username or email upon request.

I Want to delete my account, how do I do it?

  • Deleting your account is simple - simply navigate to the account settings page. Under my profile, click the “Delete Profile” Button. Enter your password, and your account will be permanently removed from our system. We cannot recover accounts deleted in this manner.

    Facebook and Twitter users must request that their account be removed from our system. Since we do not store sensitive Facebook or Twitter information, the delete function will not function without a password. We can remove the account from our back end systems.

How do I turn off emails?

  • Currently, we do not send out emails for advertising or promotional purposes. Our email system is strictly for account or ordering our products. These actions require explicit notification to our customers, and thus will not be disabled. All emails you receive are one time in nature, and will not clutter up your inbox - we promise.
