General Questions

Our membership program

  • Our membership program was built for hockey players. We want to take the pain out of ordering sticks online, and make the process simple. Being a member means you get special discounts on our sticks so you can save money.

How do our sticks compare?

  • We source from the same vendors as name brand sticks which are manufactured in the same manner, but without the added cost. Our sticks our engineered to have the same reliability, same flex, and same blade curves as the ones you already use. The only different is price; we have worked with our vendors to get the most reasonable price available in order to deliver you reasonable priced yet great quality sticks.

Shipping Questions

Shipping

  • We have partnered with Amazon in order to ensure the most reliable, fastest delivery of your sticks imaginable.

Returns

  • Sometimes sticks break, or they are shipped to you in an undesirable state. We get that. We make returns as painles as possible. Simply log into your account, click the returns tab, and submit the order number. We will refund your account the shipping cost, and send you a new stick at no cost to you.
