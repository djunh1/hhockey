Hopewell Hockey Warranty Claims


Hopewell Hockey stands behind its promise for quality with a 30 day warranty on our sticks. We understand that the game can get physical, and that sticks will break suddenly.

The warranty details are as follows:

We offer our warranties within the defined warranty period - 30 days from the date of delivery with no exceptions. Our warranty covers broken sticks where there was a defect in manufacturing. It does not cover small cracks, chips or abrasions.

In order to make a claim - please complete the form below and send an email. We only accept email and do not accept emails through a social media platform including both Facebook or Twitter.



Completing the claim


Please send us an email at info@hopewellhockey.com with the following information provided in the email. (Note, use “HOPEWELL Hockey Warranty Claim” in the subject line)

1. First and Last name.

2. A contact phone number and email.

3. The order number for the stick that you purchased.

4. The stick model purchased.

5. The failure that occurred (i.e broken blade, broken upper shaft etc.)

6. How the stick failed ( i.e Slap shot, boards, etc.)

7. Attach a photo of the damaged stick.

A customer service representative will respond to you in 48 to 72 hours. We will get your claim reviewed and processed as quickly as possible
