The Hopewell Hockey Company

Our Story

The still morning air. The crunch of ice below your skates. Bus trips with the boys. Some of our fondest memories took place on that 85 by 200 foot rectangular sheet. Hockey is our heritage.


Unfortunately, hockey keeps getting more expensive. Every year it all costs more, meaning even more families are priced out of the game. It doesn't have to be this way.


We started Hopewell Hockey because we believe that every player should have access to the best equipment. To make this happen, we engineered a superior hockey stick and decided to sell it directly to our customers. By saving on distribution and marketing costs, we are helping to make the highest performance products available to everyone.


By doing things a little differently, we will make hockey affordable for every family.


-The Hopewell Hockey Team
