A little about us

Heritage Hockey was borne on the idea that the equipment we use is expensive. We keep our gear for as long as we can, and replace sticks only when they loose their pop or if they break. Why? Because sticks run anywhere from $200 to $300 at the local pro shop. Whats worse is that the improvement year over year isn't really noticable. Wouldn't it be nice to be able to try different sticks at a price that won't cut into our budgets?

I love this idea -- Jeff Bezos

Heritage Hockey isn't just an online store to buy quality products at reasonable prices. No. We offer a membership to get sticks on a regular basis at a cost that won't bankrupt you. We source high quality, battle tested sticks and are able to offer them to you at a reasonable price. No more worrying about breaking your favorite twig and having to drop another month's rent on a new one. Browse our selection of sticks, check what other members are using, and select what you want. Your new Heritage Hockey stick will be shipped to you or to your local rink in no time. For real.
