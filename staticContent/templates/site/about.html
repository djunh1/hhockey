Who we are

The still morning air.  The Crunch of ice below your skates.  Bus trips with the boys.  Some of our fondest memories took place on that eighty-five by two-hundred foot rectangular sheet.  Hockey is our Heritage.


Unfortunatly these days, hockey has become expensive.  Each year -  the ice time, the team fees, the equipment -  they all cost more.  every year, more and more familys are priced out of the game.  It doesn't have to be this way.


At Hopewell Hockey, we don't believe in comprimise.  We beleive that every player should have access to the best equipment.  Our goal is to make the highest performance products available to everyone.  By doing things a little differently, we'll make hockey affordable for every family.


We are starting by making superior composite hockey sticks.   This is only the beginning.  It's going to be an exciting ride.  We hope you'll come along with us .


-The Hopewell Hockey Team
